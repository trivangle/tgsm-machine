Concept Summary

TGSM organizes systemic movement into structural energy layers and persistence signals, showing where risk concentrates, when stability is genuine, and how patterns evolve across windows and scales. It interprets directional energy and regime shifts as changes in risk position and momentum—whether accelerating, reducing, or holding steady. The focus is clarity—trustable signals, decisions you can explain at a glance, and confidence that scales across conditions and time horizons.
